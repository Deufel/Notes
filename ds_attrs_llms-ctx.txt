### Common Modifier Patterns

**Timing modifiers** (used by many attributes):
- `__delay.500ms` / `__delay.1s`
- `__debounce.500ms` / `__debounce.1s` + `.leading` / `.notrailing`
- `__throttle.500ms` / `__throttle.1s` + `.noleading` / `.trailing`

**Case modifiers**: `__case.camel` (default for signals), `__case.kebab` (default for classes/events), `__case.snake`, `__case.pascal`
